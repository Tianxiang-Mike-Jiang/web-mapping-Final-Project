Snapshot of NBA

There are 30 teams in the NBA that are evenly split between the East and West conferences. 29 of the NBA teams are based in the USA, with one solitary Canadian team - the Toronto Raptors. California has four NBA teams (which is the most in any state) and Texas has three. 11 of the NBA teams share their arena's with NHL teams, while the Staples Center in Los Angeles is the home to three teams; the LA Lakers, the LA Clippers and the LA Kings of the NHL. NBA game attendances range from 14,000 up to 21,000 at the United Center, home of the Chicago Bulls.
